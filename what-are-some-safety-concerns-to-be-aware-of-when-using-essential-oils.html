The properties of essential oils are what make them so useful in so many applications. But with great power comes great responsibility. There are numerous benefits of using essential oils, but only as long as they are used in moderation and in the right application. Essential oils are used to promote health and well-being. There is a growing interest in the use of essential oils for a variety of purposes, including aromatherapy, massage, and skin care. Here are some popular essential oils to use:

  • Eucalyptus: Eucalyptus essential oil has a fresh, minty aroma. It can also be used to relieve muscle pain and tension.
