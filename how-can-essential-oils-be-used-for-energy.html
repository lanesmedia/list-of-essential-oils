What are essential oils?

Essential oils are plant-based liquids that are highly concentrated forms of fragrant compounds found in various plants. They are usually derived from flowers, leaves, roots, and other parts of plants and can be found in a wide variety of products, including soaps, cleaning liquids, candles, and essential oil blends. There are thousands of different essential oils available, so it can be confusing trying to figure out what’s in which one.There are three basic types of essential oils: volatile, fixed, and absolute. Volatile essential oils are highly concentrated liquids that evaporate quickly, which is why they are used for their ability to instantly freshen a room or repel pests. Fixed essential oils are more appropriate for long-term use, such as making a skin treatment or a cleaning product. Absolute essential oils are made by using a chemical process to lock the oil in place. They can be highly toxic and are not recommended for use in any type of product.
