Where can you buy essential oils?

Youve heard all about essential oils for health and beauty, but do you know where to buy them? It can be a bit confusing picking out your first bottle because there are so many brands and variations on what constitutes an essential oil. So what makes a good essential oil and which ones are worth your money? To find out, we reached out to our friends over at the International Fragrance Marketing Association (IFMA), whose job it is to assist the industry in developing best practices and best marketing protocols for the essential oil market. They provided us with this list of suggestions for where you can buy essential oils.

Online Stores

These are great places to start if youre just getting started with essential oils. Online retailers often have a wide selection of oils, including ones that arent always easy to find in person. There are even a few websites that specialize in selling high-quality, therapeutic-grade essential oils. You can also find a lot of useful information about the benefits of each oil, possible uses, and recipes for making your own personalized blends. If you dont want to spend the money on one of these online retailers, you can also check out websites that sell wholesale bottles of essential oils. These sites usually have lower prices, but often sell lower-quality and less effective oils.

Health Food Stores

You can find essential oils almost anywhere, but if youre looking for a more specialized selection, health food stores are a great place to start. Youll find the most varieties and quality of essential oils in health food stores, but youll also pay a little more for them.Health food stores are also a great place to find organic essential oils, which are not only more healthful but also more effective. These oils are made from plants that have been raised without the use of pesticides and other chemicals, making them gentler on your skin and more suitable for sensitive skin types.

Natural Food Stores

Natural food stores are a great place to start if you want to buy organic, unprocessed oils. Theyre also a good place to find essential oils that are made from plants that dont grow well in other climates.While most essential oils are suitable for people living in temperate climates, you can also find ones appropriate for tropical or subtropical locations.

Natural Food Markets

Natural food markets are great if youre looking for something a little more obscure. You may be able to find essential oils that are made from plants native to your area. You might also be able to find oils that are made from plants that grow in other parts of the world but are only harvested during certain seasons.If youre willing to dig around a little, you can also find some great deals on essential oils at these markets. You may even find someone selling oils from their own garden.

Organic Stores

Organic essential oil stores are great for anyone who wants to buy the healthiest oils available. Theyre also a good place to find oils that have a lot of therapeutic properties.Organic essential oil stores are also a great place to find oils that are made from non-native plants, since these plants are less likely to harm the environment. These oils are also less likely to cause allergic reactions in sensitive people.

Trade Shops

Trade shops are another great place to find essential oils that arent commonly found in other stores. Youll probably find more obscure oils, like ones made from plants that grow in deserts or plants that only grow at certain times of the year.If youre willing to dig around, you can also find some great deals at trade shops. You may even find someone selling oils from their own garden.

Brick and Mortar Stores

Brick and mortar stores are great for people who want to learn more about the oils theyre buying. You can find a lot of specialized information at these stores, including information on recipes, how to use the oils, and their health benefits.Some stores also offer classes where you can learn how to make your own essential oil recipes. Brick and mortar stores are also a good place to find rare oils that are harder to find in other stores.

Conclusion

If youre looking for a great place to buy essential oils, you cant go wrong with any of the options listed above. For the best selection and quality, try online stores or health food stores, but if youre looking for something a little different, try trading shops or other specialty shops.

What are Essential Oils Good For?
Essential oils are used to promote health and well-being. There is a growing interest in the use of essential oils for a variety of purposes, including aromatherapy, massage, and skin care. Here are some popular essential oils to use:
  • Lavender: Often used for relaxation and stress relief, lavender essential oil can also be used to soothe minor cuts and scrapes.
  • Peppermint: Peppermint essential oil is invigorating and refreshing. It can also be used to relieve headaches and nausea.
  • Eucalyptus: Eucalyptus essential oil has a fresh, minty aroma. It can also be used to relieve muscle pain and tension.
If you're new to essential oils, it's best to start with a small bottle of each oil. That way, you can experiment with different combinations of oils to find what works best for you.
